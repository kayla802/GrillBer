FAQs

Do I need to clean the grill?: Yes, you should totally clean the grill after use with the provided tools, additional fees may apply for grills that have not been cleaned.

Do you supply the gas/charcoal/woodchips? All propane grills will have the tank included, any charcoal or wood grills will require the GrillBer user to supply thier own fuel source. Check-in soon for these items for purchase along with your rental

Do I need to provide anything for the person that drops off/picks-up the grill?: This is optional, if you are willing you can tip (food is always a welcome tip)
