FAQs

How do I set up these services?

Just search and click the grill you want to rent and once you set up your request the grill owner will drop off the grill at the specified time and location

Do I need to clean the grill?

Yes, you should totally clean the grill after use with the provided tools, additional fees may apply for grills that have not been cleaned.

Do you supply the gas/charcoal/woodchips?

All propane grills will have the tank included, any charcoal or wood grills will require the GrillBer user to supply thier own fuel source. Check-in soon for these items for purchase along with your rental

Do I need to provide anything for the person that drops off/picks-up the grill?

This is optional, if you are willing you can tip (food is always a welcome tip)

Are there plans to make a mobile app?

Yes. We’re glad you love GrillBer so much! Brainstorming begins March 2nd with a planned release of Spring 2021.

If I created a homemade grill, can I offer it to be rented?

Absolutely not. Only major brands can be used to offer as acceptable rental options.

How do I add myself into the GrillBer system?

You can enter your new user information in the automatic pop-up window or click on sign up in the menu bar! Glad that you are joining our family of “Grillfriends”!

How do I remove my grill from the database?

We get it. Why let other people enjoy the feeling of getting heat-kissed by the scorch of hot metal bars when you can have that moment to yourself? If you go to the “My Grills” tab under the Grills dropdown in the menu bar, you can click “Delete Grill”.

What about propane tanks?

Great Question. The expectation is that when a gas grill is being rented the propane tank is at least half full and has a propane gauge included in the rental.
