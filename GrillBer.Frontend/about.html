ABOUT

Why:

It's all the perks of owning a grill without having to worry about the large cost.

Who:

Grillber is made up of a powerful and efficiently sized group of indivuals wanting to make your rental experience a smooth one. Ryan Martin, Leigh Ann Majkowski, Kayla Clark, and Andrew Camp collectively make up the GrillBer team. They have spent 9 months to fully develop the idea and narrow in on the cutting edge technology of today's world. When they are not coding, they spend their time together singing their favorite John Mayer songs, fighting about what defines something as a sandwhich, and sharing funny kid/dog stories.

When:

The concept of GrillBer began Summer 2019 and was released to the world Late Feb 2020.

Where:

The GrillBer headquarters is based in Columbia, MO; home of the Missouri Tigers, True/False Flm Festival, and Sparky's Ice Cream. :)
